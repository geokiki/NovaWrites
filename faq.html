Some Questions You Might Have

What services does Novawrites Limited offer?

We offer a range of writing assistance services, including Personal Statements, Statements of Purpose, Recommendation Letters, Cold Emails, Academic CV Revamps, and Scholarship Essays.

What are the different service packages available?

We offer several flexible service packages:
1. Individual Services: Select specific services to meet your immediate needs.
2. Comprehensive Package: Includes both a Statement of Purpose or Personal Statement and an Academic CV overhaul.
3. Complete Application Assistance: This all-in-one package covers School Search and Selection, Academic CV Preparation, Crafting Personal Statements and Statements of Purpose, Writing and sending Cold Emails & Inquiry Letters, Drafting Recommendation Letters, and Writing Essays for Scholarships (UK focus).
4. Full Application Management: We handle the entire process from document preparation to application submission

How many schools can Novawrites Limited help me apply to?

We assist clients in applying to a minimum of 3 and a maximum of 4 schools.

How does the Complete Application Assistance package work?

This package includes:
. School Search and Selection: We help you identify the best schools that match your goals.
. Academic CV Preparation: We revamp your CV to highlight your achievements and experiences.
. Personal Statements and Statements of Purpose: We craft compelling statements tailored to each of your chosen schools.
. Cold Emails & Inquiry Letters: We draft and send professional inquiry letters to potential advisors or departments.
. Recommendation Letters: We assist in drafting strong recommendation letters.
. Scholarship Essays: We help write essays specifically for scholarship applications, focusing on UK opportunities.

What is included in the Full Application Management package?

This package provides ultimate convenience by taking charge of the entire application process. We handle everything from document preparation to application submission, ensuring that all steps are completed professionally on your behalf

Can I choose only the services I need instead of a full package?

Yes, our Individual Services package allows you to select specific services to meet your immediate needs without opting for a full package.

How do I get started with Novawrites Limited?

You can get started by contacting us through our website. We'll discuss your needs and help you choose the best service package for your goals.

What makes Novawrites Limited different from other academic assistance services?

At Novawrites Limited, we provide personalized and comprehensive support tailored to your unique needs. Our team is dedicated to helping you achieve your academic goals through professional writing assistance and full application management.
