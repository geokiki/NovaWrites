Meet Eunice Unuofin: The Visionary Behind Novawrites Limited

Eunice Unuofin has built a remarkable career as the founder and CEO of Novawrites Limited, a company dedicated to helping students achieve their academic dreams. Her journey began at a young age, driven by her own challenges and experiences in the world of higher education.

The Birth Of Novawrites Limited

Eunice's story is one of resilience and determination. As a young undergraduate, she applied to more than 20 universities in the UK, USA, and Turkey. Her hard work paid off, as she received admissions and up to 50% scholarships from these prestigious institutions. However, the lack of funding to cover the remaining 50% of her expenses prevented her from pursuing her studies abroad.

Undeterred, Eunice immersed herself in learning everything she could about scholarships and admissions. She joined numerous online groups, watched countless tutorial videos, and connected with experts in the field. During this journey, she noticed a troubling pattern: many students with outstanding qualifications were missing out on opportunities due to a lack of information and guidance.

Determined to make a difference, Eunice founded Novawrites Limited. What began as an academic writing company has now evolved into a comprehensive academic assistance service provider, dedicated to helping students secure the admissions and scholarships they deserve.

Our Mission

At Novawrites Limited, we believe that every student, regardless of their background, deserves a chance to reach their academic potential. Our mission is to provide the guidance and support necessary to help you navigate the complex world of university applications and scholarships at very affordable prices.
